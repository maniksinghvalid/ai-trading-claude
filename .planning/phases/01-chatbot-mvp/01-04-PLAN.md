<objective>
Slice 3: Add SQLite-backed conversation persistence so chat is multi-turn — `/chat` loads prior
history and appends both user and assistant turns, and `/sessions` endpoints expose the transcript.

Purpose: Enable coreference — a follow-up resolves the prior ticker without the user restating it.
Output: A `session_store` with `Turn`, history-aware `/chat`, and `/sessions` listing/detail.
</objective>
